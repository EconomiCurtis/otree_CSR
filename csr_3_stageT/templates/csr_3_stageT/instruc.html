In this part, you will first be assigned to be a Role A participant or a Role F participant. You will be informed of your role through the computer screen and you will keep the same role throughout the entire study.

Every Role A participant will be paired with a Role F participant throughout the study.

Role A participants will be told how much their Role F participant contributed (overall on average) to the Group Exchange in Part 2.

The Role A participant will first make a decision. He or she will choose between A1 and A2.

  • A1 means the round ends and both participants again receive the amount they earned during Part 2.
  • A2 means Role F will be asked to increase his or her contribution over and above what s/he contributed to the Investment Account in Part 2.

If the Role A participant chooses A2, then the Role F participant makes the next decision:

  • F1 means increasing his or her contribution to the investment account in Part 2 by 1.5 times the original contribution. Then, the round ends
  • F2 means maintaining his or her original contribution to the investment account. The Role A participant will be asked to make another decision.

You are about to start Part 2 of the study.

In this part of the study you will make a series of 10 investment decisions. For each investment decision you will be placed in a group with three other participants.

One of these 10 investment decision will be randomly selected to determine the amount of points you have to work with in the following part of the experiment.

Your investment returns will depend on the investment decision that you and the other members of your group make.

Each investment decision you make will result in an investment return. Your investment return from each decision will be turned into cash earnings.

You have been assigned to a group of 4 people.

Each of you will be given an investment account with the amount of points earned in Part 1 in it. You earned {{ret_score}} points in Part 1.

In each round, you will divide your points between two investment opportunities: the Individual Exchange and the Group Exchange.

The Individual Exchange

Every point you invest in the Individual Exchange will yield you a return of one and will reduce the earnings of the other players by 1/2 point each.

The Group Exchange

Every point you invest in the Group Exchange yields a return of 1/2 point for you. The other members of your group are not affected by your investment in the Group Exchange.

0 points 0 points 0 points
30 points 15 points 0 points
60 points 30 points 0 points

Automatic Earnings

In addition to the earnings you accumulate from the Individual Exchange and the Group Exchange, you will also get Automatic Earningseach round. These earnings will not depend on any decisions you make, and will be the same each round.

Your automatic earnings will be 120 points in each round. Hence, your total earnings in each round will be sum of your earnings from the Individual Exchange, the Group Exchange and the Automatic Earnings.

The Investment Decision

Your task is to invest all your points in the Individual Exchange and the Group Exchange.

You are free to divide the points between the two Exchanges as you wish. You can also put all of them into the Group Exchange or all of them into the Individual Exchange.

The Investment Account

You and every other member of your group will have 60 points in your investment account each decision round.

The total number of points in your group in every decision round is 240 points.

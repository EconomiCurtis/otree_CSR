Each member of your group has 60 points available to invest.

How many points would you earn if you invest 30 points into the Individual Exchange, and 30 points into the Group Exchange?

Assume that the other members place all their points in the Group Exchange.

Correct!

You earn 1 point for each point you invest into the Individual Exchange, 1/2 point for each point you invest into the Group Exchange, and 1/2 point for each point that your other group members invested into the Group Exhange:

Individual Exchange: 30 points
Group Exchange: 105 points
Your total score: 135 points
